# Mentor Character Bible

Date: 2026-05-24

## General direction

All mentors should feel wise, kind, readable, and safe for a child. They should not look angry, sharp, threatening, or sarcastic. Each mentor needs at least one clear gameplay sprite that can stand near the path. A second pose for dialog is recommended after the first production pass.

Recommended sprite set per mentor:

- `idle_talk`: calm standing or perched pose for the gameplay scene and dialog start.
- `seated_listening`: optional quieter pose for dialog or solved-question feedback.
- `emotion_variation`: optional happy/proud expression for correct answers or level completion.

## Level 1: Морская чайка

- Level: 1, `Льдина`
- Animal: sea gull.
- Age impression: adult, alert, friendly guide.
- Character: cheerful first helper, quick-eyed, encouraging.
- Visual image: white and pale-gray gull with soft wing shape, small warm beak accent, no sharp aggressive angles.
- Pose: standing on a small snow-covered ice edge, head slightly tilted toward the bear.
- Sprite need: one idle/talk sprite first; optional wing-lift happy variation later.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, optional `emotion_variation`.

## Level 2: Бобр

- Level: 2, `Ледяная река`
- Animal: beaver.
- Age impression: adult craftsperson, calm and practical.
- Character: builder, patient, explains pairs through bridges and branches.
- Visual image: warm brown beaver with rounded body, small branch or log detail, friendly eyes.
- Pose: standing or sitting beside a little branch bridge.
- Sprite need: one full-body idle/talk sprite; seated/listening pose fits well.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, `seated_listening`, optional proud smile.

## Level 3: Песец

- Level: 3, `Заснеженный берег`
- Animal: arctic fox.
- Age impression: adult but lively, clever and gentle.
- Character: curious, observant, helps notice groups and tracks.
- Visual image: fluffy white fox with soft tail curl, pale blue shadow accents, kind face.
- Pose: sitting on snow with tail wrapped near paws or standing lightly on the shore.
- Sprite need: one idle/talk sprite; seated/listening variation recommended.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, `seated_listening`, optional curious expression.

## Level 4: Сова

- Level: 4, `Северный лес`
- Animal: owl.
- Age impression: older, wise, warm, unhurried.
- Character: quiet teacher, watches carefully, speaks simply.
- Visual image: snowy owl or polar-toned owl with rounded feathers and big soft eyes.
- Pose: perched on a low snowy branch or stump at bear height.
- Sprite need: one perched idle/talk sprite; optional blink or wing-settle variation.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, optional `emotion_variation`.

## Level 5: Тюлень

- Level: 5, `Ледяная пещера`
- Animal: seal.
- Age impression: adult, kind, relaxed.
- Character: playful but calm, helps discover patterns in the cave.
- Visual image: rounded gray seal with soft whiskers, light belly, friendly face.
- Pose: resting on a smooth icy platform, head raised toward the bear.
- Sprite need: one idle/talk sprite; seated/listening can be the same base pose.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, optional happy whisker expression.

## Level 6: Северный олень

- Level: 6, `Снежная долина`
- Animal: reindeer.
- Age impression: adult, gentle, steady.
- Character: patient pathfinder, confident without pressure.
- Visual image: soft brown and cream reindeer with rounded antlers, scarf or snow charm optional.
- Pose: standing calmly on a snowy trail, head lowered slightly to speak with the bear.
- Sprite need: one full-body idle/talk sprite; optional listening pose.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, `seated_listening`, optional proud nod.

## Level 7: Овцебык

- Level: 7, `Горный перевал`
- Animal: musk ox.
- Age impression: older, strong, protective.
- Character: steady mountain guide, patient with hard steps.
- Visual image: shaggy brown musk ox with rounded horns and soft eyes; avoid making the horns feel threatening.
- Pose: standing near a safe mountain trail marker.
- Sprite need: one full-body idle/talk sprite; optional calm seated/resting pose.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, optional `seated_listening`.

## Level 8: Волк

- Level: 8, `Полярная ночь`
- Animal: wolf.
- Age impression: adult, quiet, trustworthy.
- Character: night guide, calm and protective, never scary.
- Visual image: gray-white wolf with relaxed ears, soft muzzle, warm lantern light accent nearby.
- Pose: sitting or standing in a relaxed side-facing pose, watching the path.
- Sprite need: one idle/talk sprite; seated/listening strongly recommended to keep the mood gentle.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, `seated_listening`, optional warm smile.

## Level 9: Нарвал

- Level: 9, `Северное сияние`
- Animal: narwhal.
- Age impression: adult, magical, gentle.
- Character: wonder-filled mentor, explains patterns like aurora waves.
- Visual image: pale narwhal with soft blue shadows and a rounded tusk; the tusk should feel elegant, not sharp.
- Pose: partly emerging from a calm ice pool or water opening beside the path.
- Sprite need: one idle/talk sprite with water base; optional happy sparkle expression.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, optional `emotion_variation`.

## Level 10: Белая медведица

- Level: 10, `Северный океан`
- Animal: polar bear mother.
- Age impression: adult, warm, protective, wise.
- Character: final mentor and emotional goal, proud of the cub.
- Visual image: larger white polar bear with soft features, gentle eyes, warm highlight accents.
- Pose: standing or sitting on a large floe, turned toward the cub.
- Sprite need: one full-body idle/talk sprite; second embrace/listening or proud pose recommended for finale.
- Recommended assets: `idle_talk`, `seated_listening`, optional final happy variation.
