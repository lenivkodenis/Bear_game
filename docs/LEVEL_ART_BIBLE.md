# Level Art Bible

Date: 2026-05-24

## Naming recommendation

Use `assets/data/levels.json` as the current canonical source for level names. Keep level 1 as `Льдина` and level 6 as `Снежная долина`.

The existing `snowy_clearing` art should not silently redefine level 1 as `Снежная долина`, because `Снежная долина` already exists as level 6. Production should either revise the current art so level 1 reads clearly as an ice floe, or intentionally reassign/rename that art in a separate approved content pass.

## Level 1: Льдина

- Canonical name: `Льдина`
- Location description: a safe first ice floe surrounded by soft snow, tiny ice pieces, fish, and simple landmarks for the first steps.
- Visual mood: welcoming, bright, beginner-safe.
- Main colors: snow white, pale ice blue, soft turquoise, very light sky blue.
- Background: clear northern sky, distant low ice shapes, gentle horizon.
- Midground: rounded ice floes, small snow mounds, simple safe path marks.
- Foreground: main walkable snowy ice surface with small fish, shells, or snow pebbles near the edges.
- Obstacles: tiny cracks, low snow bumps, small ice blocks; all should look harmless.
- Mentor: Морская чайка.
- Overall vibe: first lesson, one-by-one counting, safe start.

## Level 2: Ледяная река

- Canonical name: `Ледяная река`

- Visual mood: forward motion, pairs, cooperation.
- Main colors: ice blue, soft river blue, snow white, warm brown accents for branches.
- Background: distant river bend, low snowy banks, pale sky.
- Midground: frozen water channels, small bridge pieces, paired objects.
- Foreground: snow-packed riverbank with simple branch or log details.
- Obstacles: low branch bundles, small ice cracks, stepping stones.
- Mentor: Бобр.
- Overall vibe: counting in pairs, practical building, helpful guidance.
